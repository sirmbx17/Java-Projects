Our History

We are a local non Profit that provide assistance to people who have some disability. Our organization was founded in 1980 by John Rummage as a local shelter for families in poverty. Our founder John Rummage was a well respected businessman in the kansas city are and around missouri . From that point our non profit has grown in size. Today , we help thousands of families with disability around the kansas city area.

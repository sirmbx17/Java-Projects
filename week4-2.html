Rummage Sale Page

Welcome to our Rummage garage sale page.We are a local Non-profit the provide assistance to disabled people in our community. We are based in the kansas city area and serve people across the state of missouri.Since our start in 1990 , we have helped over 100,000 disabled members of our community and thier families.

Our headquarters

Our local non-proft was founded in 1990 by John Rummage.John Rummage was a well known lawyer around the kansas city area.He started the non-proft to help people in poverty get back in thier feet.Our main office is located in 18th and vine(see picture above)in downtown kansas city.Over the years,our non profit has grown in size and service we offer.Our Founder john Rummage is the current chairman of our non profit.
